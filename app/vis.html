Find the amount of renewable energy of every country in The Green Energy Race. See if your own country is going to succeed in the objectives of the European Union, where every European country has to have at least a 20% share of renewable energy consumption by 2020.

All forms of energy in this visualization are defined in Terajoule, which corresponds to 1012 Joule.

All data shown in this visualization is collected from World Bank Open Data, which provides free and open access to data about development in countries around the globe.
